WELCOME TO THE POCKET SOCIETY PROJECT

Pocket Society is a collection of 10,000 completely unique Pocket People NFTS- 5,000 males and 5,000 females. These teeny tiny people have an entire society living on the Solana blockchain. Your pocket person not only will be a ticket to a whole new utopia but also grants access to our game that is currently in the works. Pocket Society is a RPG computer game where your NFT doubles as your own unique character. In the world of Sol, you’ll be able to fight amongst other players for loot worth real money, build an empire and go on a quest to destroy the monsters who want to tear your entire society down!
